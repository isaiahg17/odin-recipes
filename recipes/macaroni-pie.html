Macaroni Pie

Description

Still dont know how to describe a recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni, uncooked
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 ⅔ cups evaporated milk, (measure from 2 cans of Carnation milk)
  • 3 ½ cups cheddar cheese, grated, reserve ½ cup for topping
  • ¼ teaspoon white pepper
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • salt, to taste

Make Macaroni Pie Step-by-Step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Boil macaroni in salted water approximately 8 minutes, or according to package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside
  3. In a large bowl, beat eggs until fluffy
  4. Add milk, pepper, sugar and salt and stir until combined
  5. Stir in 3 cups of cheese, and the cooked macaroni
  6. Pour into a greased, 9x11 baking dish and top with reserved ½ cup of cheese
  7. Bake until firm, 35-40 minutes
  8. Allow pie to rest for 10-15 minutes before serving
